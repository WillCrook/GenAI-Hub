Review details

ChatGPT

Strengths: Flexible dialogue, explanations and rapid idea generation.

Important limitations: Fabricated facts, uneven citations and overconfident wording.

Best practice: Supply bounded context, request uncertainty and verify against authoritative sources.

GitHub Copilot

Strengths: In-editor suggestions, boilerplate and test ideas.

Important limitations: Insecure patterns, outdated APIs and code you may not understand.

Best practice: Review diffs, run tests and use security tooling.

NotebookLM

Strengths: Exploring relationships within material you provide.

Important limitations: Incomplete source packs produce incomplete or misleading answers.

Best practice: Open the cited passage and retain your own reading notes.

Grammarly

Strengths: Quick spelling, grammar and readability suggestions.

Important limitations: Advice may conflict with disciplinary conventions or your intended voice.

Best practice: Accept changes selectively and preserve authorship.

Adobe Firefly

Strengths: Rapid visual exploration and creative variants.

Important limitations: Visual inaccuracies, bias and questions about appropriate disclosure.

Best practice: Inspect details, document generation and label AI-assisted imagery where required.

Microsoft 365 Copilot

Strengths: Assistance within familiar document and productivity tools.

Important limitations: Quality depends on available context, permissions and licence configuration.

Best practice: Check source files, permissions and every generated summary before sharing.

Information changes

Tool features, prices, terms and data-handling policies can change. Confirm current details and University guidance before choosing or purchasing a service.
